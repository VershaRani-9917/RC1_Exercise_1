Notable Awards and Honors

  • Knight Commander of the Order of the British Empire(KBE) - Bill Gates received this honor in 2002 for his contribution to the technology industry and global.
  • Presidential Medal of Freedom - In 2016, Gates was awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom by President Barak Obama for his Philanthropic efforts and impact on technology.
  • Padama Bhushan - The government of Indai honored Bill Gates with the Padama Bhushan , the country's thired-highest civilian award, in 2015 for his contribution in social work.
  • Albert Einstein Technology Medal - Gates recived this medal in 2010 for his transformative impact in software industry and his commitment to improve global health and education.
  • Legion of Honour - The French goverment awarded Bill Gates the legion of Honour in 2017 , recorgnizing his exceptional contribution to science and society.
  • Bower Award for Business Leadership - Gates recieved this award in 2010 for his leadership in the business world and his education to addressing global challenges through Philanthropy.
